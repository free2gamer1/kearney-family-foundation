Our story

Serving the Kearney community since 2003.

The Kearney Family Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit corporation founded in November 2003. Every penny donated — 100% of all donations — goes directly to the children and families we help. Volunteers and area businesses donate all of our operating costs.

Our mission

The Kearney Family Foundation is dedicated as a community of individual volunteers to assisting the children and families in the Kearney/Holt area. Our administration is handled entirely by a volunteer Board of Directors, allowing us to operate with no administrative fees so that all contributions focus on what matters most: helping others.

Every year during the holiday season, KFF and its numerous volunteers come together to benefit the citizens of Kearney, Missouri — shopping, wrapping, and delivering gifts to families in need. Our primary delivery day is the first Thursday of December each year.

Volunteers like you and area businesses donate all of our operating costs, so every dollar you give goes straight to a family.
